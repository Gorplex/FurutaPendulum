1 /** @file dox_channels.cpp
2  * @page pd_channels Channel Commands
3  * This page contains tables showing which channels are accessed by which channel
4  * commands on different versions of the PolyDAQ 2 board. These channel commands
5  * can be used in the data logger configuration file and when communicating with
6  * a PolyDAQ through the dumb terminal.
7  *
8  * All commands are @b case @b sensitive. For example, typing @b D will cause the
9  * PolyDAQ to perform an A/D conversion on the channel specified by the
10  * hexadecimal number D (decimal 13), but typing @b d will cause the PolyDAQ to
11  * show the results of a memory dump (a programming diagnostic tool).
12  *
13  * When a command is not understood by the PolyDAQ, it shows the confusing
14  * command and asks, "What's That Function?"
15  *
16  * @section pd_044 PolyDAQ 0-4-4
17  * This version of the PolyDAQ 2 board is designed for use in the ME 410 course,
18  * which makes heavy use of strain gauges. It has four strain gauge bridge
19  * amplifiers and four voltage inputs. Two of the voltage inputs accept -10 to
20  * +10V inputs and the other two accept 0 to 3.3V inputs with finer resolution.
21  * If present, an onboard MMA8452Q accelerometer provides 3 axes of acceleration
22  * data. An external accelerometer can be connected to the board's I2C bus; it
23  * will require custom firmware and commands to access.
24  *
25  * The given calibration numbers are approximate and for convenience only. The
26  * user should check all calibrations carefully using a high-accuracy voltmeter.
27  * In many cases, entering a slope of 1.0 and offset of 0.0 to cause raw A/D
28  * output numbers to be recorded is the best option.
29  *
30  * | Channel | Command | Slope | Offset | Description |
31  * |:-------:|:-------:|:-----:|:------:|:------------|
32  * | @c S1 | @c E | 0.00806 (1) | (2) | Strain gauge bridge 1 |
33  * | @c S2 | @c F | 0.00806 (1) | (2) | Strain gauge bridge 2 |
34  * | @c S3 | @c 9 | 0.00806 (1) | (2) | Strain gauge bridge 3 |
35  * | @c S4 | @c 8 | 0.00806 (1) | (2) | Strain gauge bridge 4 |
36  * | @c V1 | @c A | 0.00513 | -8.8537 | Voltage channel 1 (-10 to +10V) |
37  * | @c V2 | @c B | 0.00513 | -8.8537 | Voltage channel 2 (-10 to +10V) |
38  * | @c V3 | @c C | 0.000806 | 0.0 | Voltage channel 3 (0 to +3.3V) |
39  * | @c V4 | @c D | 0.000806 | 0.0 | Voltage channel 4 (0 to +3.3V) |
40  * | @c X | @c X | 0.000061 (3) | 0.0 | Accelerometer X axis |
41  * | @c Y | @c Y | 0.000061 (3) | 0.0 | Accelerometer Y axis |
42  * | @c Z | @c Z | 0.000061 (3) | 0.0 | Accelerometer Z axis |
43  *
44  * Notes: <br>
